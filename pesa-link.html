Pesalink

PesaLink is an interbank money transfer solution that enables you to receive funds from other banks straight into your Equity Bank Account in real time. Through PesaLink you can receive money into your bank account using a mobile phone number or Bank Account number. PesaLink is currently available to all Equity Bank customers on the Equitel Menu, My Money, Send Money, To Others option.

Why Pesalink

It is instant. Money reflects in your Bank Account in real time.

Pay for goods and services - Customers linked to PesaLink can pay in any outlet displaying the PWE sign.

Allows you to receive money into your account using your phone number.

Available 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

Enables cash transfers from Kshs10 to Kshs 999,999.

Enables transfer of foreign currencies.

Requirements

  • You must register with PesaLink to receive money into your Equity Bank Account using your Equitel mobile number.
  • You can only register one mobile number to one Account Number. If you have multiple accounts with other banks, you can use other mobile numbers to register into those accounts.
  • One must accept the terms and conditions before using the service.

How To;

1. Go to Equitel menu

2. Select My Money

3. Select PesaLink Registration

4. Select Register

5. Select Account to Register

6. Enter Alias

7. Confirm details & accept terms

8. Enter PIN & submit

9. You will receive an SMS confirmation
