Get an Equitel line from any Equity Branch and enjoy Free Equitel to Equitel money transfers.

Buying Airtime

There are various ways you can purchase Equitel airtime:

Purchase directly from your bank account using Equitel: Go to Equitel Menu>>My Money>>Buy Buy Airtime/ Bundles.

Purchase from MPESA balance via Mpesa Paybill 41 22 22 (The Equitel number is the account number).

Purchase using cash at any Equity bank agent, Pay with Equity (PWE) merchant or Equity branch.

Sign up for the Equitel Auto top-up service where airtime will automatically be loaded to your Equitel mobile phone number on a monthly basis. Subscribers of this service enjoy a special calling rate of Kes. 3.00 per minute across all networks.
